Who Are We?

From the beginning, our vision has been for Coffee House 29 to collaborate with others to support the community and help those in need.

The coffee house is designed with meetings in mind, from tete-a-tete twosomes to a private conference space with a table large enough to seat 14. With a paneled tongue-in-groove vaulted ceiling, plus a double-sided fireplace shared with the coffee house’s main seating area, the space feels more like a mountain lodge than corporate boardroom. All that’s needed to seal the lodge look is a snowstorm!
